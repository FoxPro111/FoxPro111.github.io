Perry Contini
Art Director

When it comes to CLC Perry is motivated and animated by a single principle:

We exist to help our clients build their businesses, to help them become more profitable.

For more than two decades Perry and his colleague-employees have honed a laser focus on helping clients thrive. Sharing the management reins with wife and CEO Paula, the Continis have built an agency with regional and national clients across the country and employees in six major hubs.

Perry and Paula are most proud of what they have been able to build and sustain within an always fluctuating economy and the sea-change brought on by the internet, digital marketing and social platforms that have altered the game forever.

So what’s next? More of the same, just better.

Continue to attract great people who share CLC’s reason for being and the client-partners who allow us to do great work on their behalf.

And through it all, we’ll enjoy the journey and the wonderful people we meet along the way.
