About

Founded in 1996 by husband and wife, Paula and Perry Contini, CLC advertising has been built brick by brick into an autonomous machine performing at an elite level.

A certified Woman Business Enterprise (WBE), CLC is based in Pittsburgh and currently operation with 25 employees with locations in New York, Atlanta, Washington D.C. and Cincinnati. With clients such as Sears, Firestone, Koons, Automotive group, Owens corning, Penn State, Liberty University and Power X, CLC now operates on a national level.
